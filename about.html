Trade offers

Trade offers allow residents to use their earned personal points to exchange goods and services within the community. Instead of one-to-one bartering, the point system makes trades simple and fair.

Green actions

Green actions are eco-friendly habits that residents can do daily, like biking, recycling, or using reusable bags. Each action adds to the community's climate points.

Milestones

Milestones represent shared goals of the community, such as cleaning a park or planting trees. They encourage teamwork and reward participation with points.
